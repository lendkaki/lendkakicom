# Impeccable design context

Design context for lendkaki.com. Used by Impeccable skills and commands to keep UI consistent with brand and goals.

---

## Design Context

### Users
- **Who:** Loan seekers in need of quick loans regardless of financial position. Target low/middle income now; high income in the future.
- **Context:** Marketing to the masses; mobile and desktop. Fuss-free, quick, seamless experience is the priority.
- **Job to be done:** Get a quick loan with minimal friction; understand Lendkaki, trust the platform, and take action.
- **Emotions to evoke:** Friendly, confident, minimalistic and sleek/techy — never cold or corporate.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice & tone:** Friendly but minimalistic and sleek/techy.
- **3-word personality:** Fast, efficient, modern lending.
- **Reference sites / “right feel”:** [Targon](https://targon.com/), [Pera Wallet](https://perawallet.app/), [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/) — for its clean section structure and layout clarity only (not editorial style). Typography drives the feel: minimalistic, modern, strong white space, varied sections (e.g. bento grids). Accent used like Targon’s blue — one clear accent (yellow) on black/white base.
- **Anti-references:** Bad typography; generic WordPress look. Avoid looking templated or dated.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone:** Minimal, bold, modern, with elements of playfulness to keep it friendly for the masses.
- **Theme:** Light only.
- **Colors:** Black / white base with yellow accent. Accent: `#f7d347`. Use yellow for CTAs, highlights, and key UI; ensure sufficient contrast on white (e.g. pair with dark text or borders where needed).

### Design Principles
1. **Fast & seamless** — Every step should feel quick and fuss-free; no unnecessary friction.
2. **Typography-led** — Strong, intentional type hierarchy and white space; typography defines the modern, minimal feel.
3. **Friendly but sleek** — Approachable and playful touches without sacrificing a minimal, techy look.
4. **Clear structure** — Varied sections and layout (e.g. bento grids) to create rhythm and avoid flat, generic pages.
5. **One accent** — Black/white/yellow only; use yellow purposefully and with good contrast.
